CallVia AI
CallVia AI is a human-like AI agent platform built for insurance agents, brokers, and companies, automating prospect outreach, rapport-building, and appointment scheduling. Powered by Lovable and Retell AI, CallVia AI can follow up with contacts via SMS, gather qualifying information, and automatically book qualified leads directly into calendar systems. With integrated Stripe payments, businesses can seamlessly pay for AI-driven services and track agent performance using Chatdash dashboards.
Features:
	•	Automated AI-powered follow-ups and lead nurturing tailored for insurance and sales professionals.
	•	Real-time scheduling of appointments directly into client calendars.
	•	Context-aware, empathetic AI conversations for increased engagement and qualification.
	•	End-to-end Stripe payment integration for service monetization.
	•	Client and agent management dashboard via Chatdash for easy monitoring and scalability.
	•	Built using modern, scalable AI prompt workflows with n8n automations and third-party integrations.
This platform streamlines lead conversion and appointment booking, saving time and boosting productivity for sales teams in the insurance industry
